Hamza Mukhtar


I am a research scientist in Computer Vision and Machine Learning Lab (CVML) at Al-Khawarizmi Institute of Computer Science (KICS) and National Center of Artificial Intelligence working on Computer vision, generative modeling and LLMs. Prior to joning KICS, I worked as machine learning engineer at Sagacious. I did my MS Computer science from Lahore University of Engineering and Technology (UET) in 2023 advised by Dr. Muhammad Usman Ghani Khan. I completed my undergraduate degrees in computer science at University of Management and Technology (UMT) Lahore. While there, I did research in Machine Learning, Computer Vision and NLP advised by Dr. Shahid Mahmood Awan and Dr. Adnan Abid.

For any inquiries, feel free to reach out to me via mail!

Awards: In 2019, I selected for Rector merit and Rector Medal Award in BS Software Engineering at the University of University of Management and Technology Lahore convocation. In 2018, I selected for incubetion in National Incubation Center at Lahore University of Management Sciences. In 2017, I was selected for the dean merit ward.
